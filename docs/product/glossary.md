# Glossary

Plain-language definitions of terms you'll see around Listing Elevate.

**16:9 (horizontal)** — The widescreen format, for the MLS, websites, YouTube,
and email.

**9:16 (vertical)** — The tall format, for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories.

**Add-on** — An optional extra on an order, like voiceover or both formats.

**Approve** — Marking a delivered video as your final, ready-to-send version.

**Archive** — Hiding a video from your main library without deleting it. Fully
reversible.

**Brand kit** — Your logo, colors, and styling applied to your videos
(brokerage plans).

**Cut** — A finished version of your video. You might receive a 16:9 cut and a
9:16 cut.

**Direction / director** — The automatic step that plans each shot: what's in the
room, how the camera moves, and the order of shots. See
[How it works](./03-how-it-works.md).

**Embed** — A chrome-less, view-only player you can drop into your own website
via an iframe.

**Format** — The shape of the video: horizontal (16:9), vertical (9:16), or both.

**Life Cycle** — A three-video package covering a listing from Just Listed →
Just Pended → Just Closed.

**Magic link** — The passwordless sign-in email. Click it to log in.

**Market update** — Auto-generated blog and email content built from market data.
See [Market updates](./09-market-updates.md).

**Minor edit** — A small, included revision: reorder, trim, music swap, copy
tweak.

**Package** — The order tier: Single Listing, Five-Pack, or Brokerage.

**Preset** — A saved set of your usual order options for one-click reuse.

**Property / listing** — One home you've submitted for a video.

**Re-run** — Regenerating a video from scratch (free when the direction is off).

**Revoke** — Turning off a share link so its URL stops working.

**Share link** — A hosted watch page for your video that you send to others.
Comes in **client** and **public** kinds.

**Turnaround** — The time from finishing your upload to receiving your video:
under 24 hours.

**Voice cloning** — Recreating your own voice from a short sample for the
voiceover.

**Voiceover** — Spoken narration over the video, generated or in your own cloned
voice.
