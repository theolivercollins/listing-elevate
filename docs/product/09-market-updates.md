Beyond listing videos, Listing Elevate can turn raw market data into ready-to-
publish **market-update content** — the kind of "here's how the market did this
month" blog post and email that keep you in front of your sphere.

## What it does

You provide a market-stats report (typically a PDF from your MLS or board), pick
the regions you cover, and the system produces, for each region:

- A **blog post** summarizing the market — homes for sale, sold, pending, days on
  market, and a plain-language verdict on where the market is heading.
- A matching **email** version for your newsletter.

Each region's content is written from that region's actual numbers, so a post for
one neighborhood reads differently from another — no copy-paste sameness.

## How to use it

1. **Upload your market report** (PDF). The system reads the figures out of it.
2. **Confirm the regions** you want content for.
3. **Review the drafts** — one blog post and one email per region, with a
   featured image you can keep or replace.
4. **Publish & send** — push the blog post live and send the email, each behind a
   simple two-step confirmation so nothing goes out by accident.

## Templates

Your blog and email posts are built from **templates** with your branding and
layout. The market figures and per-region wording are filled in automatically;
your structure, tone, and design stay consistent every month.

If you'd like to change the look or wording of the template itself, talk to us —
templates are set up per account.

## Featured images

Each post comes with a featured image. You can keep the suggested one or upload
your own from the post editor.

---

Market Updates is separate from the video pipeline but lives in the same
dashboard, so your listings and your market content are managed in one place.
