## How pricing works

Listing Elevate is **priced per listing** — you pay per video, not a monthly
subscription. Price depends on:

- The **package** (single listing vs. multi-listing pack)
- The **length** of the video (15 / 30 / 60 seconds)
- Any **add-ons** (voiceover, both formats, etc.)

## Packages

| Package | Price | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| **Single Listing** | from **$65** | One listing, one video. Up to 60 photos, 16:9 and 9:16, under 24 hours, unlimited minor edits. |
| **Five-Pack** | **$275** ($55/listing) | Everything in Single, plus **voiceover included** and **priority queue**. |
| **Brokerage** | Custom | Volume pricing, brand kit on every video, and a dedicated account manager. [Talk to us.](#contact) |

Longer videos and certain format/add-on combinations adjust the price; the order
screen shows the total for your exact choices.

## What's always included

- A 16:9 cut, a 9:16 cut, or both (per your order)
- A human-reviewed edit before delivery
- Under-24-hour turnaround
- **Unlimited minor edits**, and a **free full re-run** if the direction is off
  (see [Reviewing & revisions](./05-reviewing-and-revisions.md))

## Billing & invoices

Charges are per order, based on what you selected. Your order history lives in
your dashboard. For receipts, invoices, or billing questions, contact us — see
below.
