When your video is ready, you'll find it on the listing's page under
**Properties**. Here's how to review it and request changes.

## Reviewing your cut

Open the property to watch the delivered video. You can:

- **Play it** in both formats you ordered (16:9 and/or 9:16).
- **Approve it** — mark it ready so it's clearly your final, sharable version.
- **Download it** — see [Sharing & delivery](./06-sharing-and-delivery.md).
- **Request changes** — see below.

## Minor edits (unlimited)

Small adjustments are included with every order, as many rounds as you need:

- Reorder shots
- Trim or shorten
- Swap the music
- Tweak on-screen text or the voiceover script
- Add or remove emphasis on a particular room

To request one, use the **revision / notes** control on the property and describe
what you want in plain language — for example, *"open on the pool instead of the
front door"* or *"swap to calmer music and cut the garage shot."* Our editor
applies the change and re-delivers.

## Full re-runs (free if the direction is off)

If the whole direction feels wrong — not a tweak, but a different take entirely —
we **re-run the pipeline from scratch at no charge**. Just say so in your notes
("this isn't the vibe — can we try a more luxury, slower feel?") and you'll get a
fresh cut.

## Tips for fast, accurate revisions

- **Be specific.** "Make shot 3 slower and lose the last clip" beats "make it
  better."
- **Reference rooms or moments** by what's in them ("the kitchen orbit," "the
  closing aerial").
- **Bundle your notes.** Listing everything you want in one pass is quicker than
  several one-line requests.

Once you're happy, **approve** the cut and head to
[Sharing & delivery](./06-sharing-and-delivery.md) to get it out into the world.
