# FAQ

Last updated: 2026-06-14

See also:
- [Documentation home](./README.md)
- [Troubleshooting](./12-troubleshooting.md)

The quick answers. For more depth, follow the links.

### How fast will I get my video?

Under 24 hours from the moment you finish uploading — every time. Most orders
come back well inside that window.

### What if I don't like the first cut?

You get **unlimited minor edits** — reorder, trim, swap music, tweak the copy. If
the whole direction feels off, we **re-run the pipeline free of charge**. See
[Reviewing & revisions](./05-reviewing-and-revisions.md).

### What photos do I need?

20 to 60 photos, any orientation. Phone-camera quality is fine — we handle
exposure, crop, and order. The minimum is 10. See
[Photo guidelines](./04-photo-guidelines.md).

### Portrait or landscape?

Both are available. You can order a 16:9 cut for the MLS, a 9:16 cut for
Instagram and TikTok, or both from a single upload.

### Can I add voiceover?

Yes. Generated voiceover is $15, or your own voice cloned (from a ~30-second
sample) is $25. It's included free on the Five-Pack. See
[Voiceover & add-ons](./08-voiceover-and-addons.md).

### How much does it cost?

It's priced per listing, starting at $65 for a single listing and $55 per listing
on the Five-Pack. Brokerage volume pricing is available. See
[Pricing & billing](./10-pricing-and-billing.md).

### Do I need a subscription?

No. You pay per video, not monthly.

### Can I share the video directly with my seller?

Yes — generate a **client share link**, or download the file and post it
anywhere. You can also embed it on your own site. See
[Sharing & delivery](./06-sharing-and-delivery.md).

### Is there an app to install?

No. Listing Elevate runs in your browser. Sign in with a magic link and you're in
— no password, no download.

### What happens to my old videos?

They stay in your [video library](./07-video-library.md), organized into folders,
ready to re-share any time.

### Do you do anything besides listing videos?

Yes — qualifying accounts can also generate **market-update** blog posts and
emails from market data. See [Market updates](./09-market-updates.md).
