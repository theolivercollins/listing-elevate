Ordering a video is a four-step flow: **Style → Property → Add-ons → Photos**.
You can move back and forth between steps before you submit. Here's each step.

## Step 1 — Style

Choose what kind of video you want.

**Video type:**

| Type | Use it for |
|---|---|
| **Just Listed** | A brand-new listing hitting the market |
| **Just Pended** | A property that just went under contract |
| **Just Closed** | Celebrating a successful close |
| **Life Cycle** | A three-video series across the listing's life (Just Listed → Pended → Closed). *Best value.* |

**Length:** 15, 30, or 60 seconds. Shorter cuts are punchier for social;
60 seconds gives room for a fuller walkthrough.

**Format:**

- **Horizontal (16:9)** — the standard cut for the MLS, YouTube, and websites.
- **Vertical (9:16)** — built for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories.
- **Both** — get both cuts from one order.

> Availability of vertical/both formats can vary; the order screen always shows
> what's currently offered and the exact price.

## Step 2 — Property

Enter the listing so the video is accurate and personalized.

- **Address** — start typing and pick from the autocomplete suggestions.
- **Listing details** — price, beds, baths, square footage, and similar fields.
  These can appear on-screen and inform the voiceover script.
- **MLS lookup** — where available, we can pull listing details automatically so
  you don't retype them.

Accurate details here make the final video tighter — the script and any on-screen
text are built from what you enter.

## Step 3 — Add-ons

Optional extras. Skip this step entirely if you just want the video.

- **Voiceover** — add a generated narration, or clone your own voice from a short
  sample. See [Voiceover & add-ons](./08-voiceover-and-addons.md) for voices and
  pricing.
- **Custom requests** — a free-text note for anything specific: "lead with the
  pool," "keep it calm and luxury," "don't show the garage." Our editor reads
  these before finalizing your cut.

## Step 4 — Photos

Upload the photos the video is built from.

- **How many:** 20–60 is the sweet spot (10 is the minimum).
- **Quality:** phone-camera quality is fine. We handle exposure, cropping, and
  ordering automatically.
- **Orientation:** mix portrait and landscape freely.

Drag and drop, or click to browse. You can reorder or remove photos before
submitting. Full guidance is in [Photo guidelines](./04-photo-guidelines.md).

## Submitting

Review the running price (it updates as you choose options), then **submit**.
Your listing appears under **Properties** immediately and the pipeline starts.
You can safely close the tab — see [How it works](./03-how-it-works.md).

> **Presets:** If you order often, save a **preset** of your usual style, format,
> and add-ons so future orders are one click. Look for the bookmark/save control
> on the Style step.
