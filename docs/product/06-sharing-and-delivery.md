Once your video is ready, you have a few ways to get it in front of buyers,
sellers, and your own channels.

## What you receive

Every order delivers the format(s) you chose:

- **16:9 (horizontal)** — for the MLS, your website, YouTube, and email.
- **9:16 (vertical)** — for Instagram Reels, TikTok, and Stories.

Both play in a clean, branded player — no third-party logos.

## Download

From the property page, **download** the raw video file for any format you
ordered. The downloaded MP4 is yours to upload anywhere — the MLS, social
schedulers, ad platforms, your CRM.

## Share links

Instead of (or alongside) downloading, you can generate a **share link** — a
hosted watch page for the video that you send to anyone. Share links come in two
kinds:

| Kind | Use it for |
|---|---|
| **Client link** | Sending to a seller or buyer — a polished, view-and-approve page. |
| **Public link** | Posting anywhere — social bios, listing sites, email blasts. |

For each link you can:

- **Label it** (e.g. "Seller — review copy," "Instagram") so you can tell your
  links apart later.
- **Track views** — see how many times it's been watched.
- **Revoke it** — turn off a link instantly if it shouldn't be live anymore. The
  old URL stops working.

## Embedding on your own site

Want the film on your personal listing page or website? Use the **embed** option
to drop a chrome-less, view-only player into any page that accepts an iframe. It's
single-orientation and carries no buttons — just the video, filling its frame.

## Approving for delivery

Marking a video **approved** signals it's your final, ready-to-send version. It's
a good habit before you share widely — it keeps your library tidy and makes the
"this is the one" cut unmistakable. See
[Reviewing & revisions](./05-reviewing-and-revisions.md).

## Where your videos live

Everything you've delivered is collected in **Your video library**, where you can
organize, find, and re-share past videos. See
[Your video library](./07-video-library.md).
