Most things just work. When they don't, start here.

## I can't sign in / my magic link didn't work

- Magic links **expire** after a short window. Request a fresh one and use the
  newest email — don't click an older link.
- Check spam/promotions folders for the sign-in email.
- Make sure you're entering the **same email** you signed up with.

## My upload won't submit

- You need at least **10 photos** (20–60 is ideal). Add more if you're short.
- Very large files can take a moment to finish uploading — wait for every photo
  to show as uploaded before submitting.
- All required listing fields (like the address) must be filled in. The
  **Next/Submit** button stays disabled until the current step is complete.

## My video is taking a while

Turnaround is **under 24 hours** from when your upload finishes — it's normal for
a video to still be processing for some time after you submit. Your listing shows
a live status under **Properties**. If it's been more than 24 hours, contact us.

## The video isn't quite right

That's what revisions are for:

- For a small change, request a [minor edit](./05-reviewing-and-revisions.md) —
  reorder shots, trim, swap music, adjust copy.
- If the whole direction is off, ask for a **free full re-run** in your notes.

Be specific about what to change for the fastest turnaround.

## A room I wanted isn't featured

Make sure that room had a few clear, well-lit photos in your upload (see
[Photo guidelines](./04-photo-guidelines.md)), then request a revision asking to
feature it.

## A share link isn't working

- The link may have been **revoked**, or the video may have been **deleted** from
  the library (deleting a video disables its links). Check the video in your
  [library](./07-video-library.md) and generate a fresh link if needed.
- Confirm you copied the full URL.

## Still stuck?

Reach out via in-app chat or email. Brokerage and volume accounts have a
dedicated account manager — see [Pricing & billing](./10-pricing-and-billing.md).
