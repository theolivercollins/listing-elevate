Add-ons are optional extras you choose on the **Add-ons** step when ordering. None
are required — order a video with no add-ons at all if you like.

## Voiceover

A narration track that walks a viewer through the home. You have two options:

| Option | What it is | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Generated voiceover** | A natural AI voice reads a script written from your listing details. | $15 |
| **Your own voice (cloned)** | We clone your voice from a short (~30-second) sample, so the narration sounds like you. | $25 |

> Voiceover is **included free** on the Five-Pack package. See
> [Pricing & billing](./10-pricing-and-billing.md).

### Choosing a voice

For generated voiceover, pick from a catalog of voices with different tones —
warm and polished, deep and commanding, young and conversational, and more. You
can **preview** a voice before committing, and the script is written to match your
property and the mood you asked for.

### Cloning your own voice

Choose **your own voice**, then provide a short clean recording (around 30
seconds of you speaking naturally). We use it to generate narration in your voice
for the video. The cleaner the sample — quiet room, clear speech — the better the
result.

## Custom requests

A free-text note attached to your order. Use it for anything specific the
director and editor should know, for example:

- "Lead with the pool and the view."
- "Keep it calm — luxury, slow, minimal."
- "Don't show the garage or the laundry room."
- "Match the music to a high-energy social edit."

Our editor reads custom requests before finalizing your cut, so this is the best
place to steer the feel up front rather than asking for changes afterward.

## Music

Every video includes a music track chosen to fit the mood. If you'd like
something different, ask in a custom request, or swap it as a free
[minor edit](./05-reviewing-and-revisions.md) after delivery.

## Format add-ons

Ordering **both** the 16:9 and 9:16 cuts from a single upload may carry a small
add-on fee versus a single format — the order screen always shows the exact
price as you select.
