# Your video library

Last updated: 2026-06-14

See also:
- [Sharing & delivery](./06-sharing-and-delivery.md)
- [Documentation home](./README.md)

The **video library** is the home for every video you've had made — think of it
as your own private video catalog, built into Listing Elevate. Find it under
**Videos** in your dashboard.

## The grid

Each video appears as a poster card showing:

- The property's hero photo and address
- The client/listing name
- Format badges (16:9 / 9:16)
- View count and an **approved** badge once you've signed off
- The delivery date

**Filter and search** by client and date range, or type an address to jump
straight to a listing.

## Folders

Keep things organized by grouping videos into **folders** — for example by
campaign, by team member, by year, or by neighborhood.

- **New folder** — create one from the folder rail.
- **Move a video** — open a video's menu (⋯) and choose *Move to folder*.
- **Rename / reorder** — manage folders inline from the rail.
- **Delete a folder** — the folder goes away but its videos are **not** deleted;
  they simply become unfiled. Nothing is lost.

## Archiving

Done with a video but don't want to delete it? **Archive** it. Archived videos
drop out of your main grid into the **Archived** view, where you can **restore**
them any time. Archiving is fully reversible — it's just tidying.

## Deleting a video

If you really want a video gone, use **Delete** from its menu and confirm.

> **Heads up:** Deleting a video also **stops its share links from working** —
> anyone holding a link will no longer be able to watch it. This can't be undone,
> so archive instead if you're unsure.

## Re-sharing a past video

Any video in your library can be shared again at any time — generate a fresh
[share link or embed](./06-sharing-and-delivery.md) straight from its page. Old
campaigns can be revived without re-ordering anything.
